Meet our Judges



Layla Ahmed

Layla Ahmed is a renowned Arab vocalist known for her powerful performances and versatile vocal range. With a career spanning over two decades, she has influenced a generation of aspiring artists. Layla is passionate about nurturing young talent and guiding them on their musical journey.


Sara Khouri

Sara Khouri is a celebrated choreographer and dancer who has mesmerized audiences with her graceful moves and choreographic brilliance. Her dedication to preserving cultural dance forms while infusing them with modern elements has made her a revered figure in the dance community. Sara is eager to witness the fusion of diverse dance styles in this year's contest.

Karim Nassar is a distinguished Arab music composer and producer, with numerous chart-topping hits to his name. His innovative approach to blending traditional melodies with contemporary beats has earned him accolades across the region. Karim is excited to discover fresh talent that embodies the spirit of musical evolution.
